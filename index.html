Finding the Ideal Place to Meet Your Needs

Knowing where the best place to live can be stressful. Especially for the Veteran and Military community. There are many choices for resources available, and this is one that has the Military community in mind. Designed by those who understand. Our Search page has ways to narrow down priorities, whether it be distance to a college, tech school, or VA facility. It also breaks down population, median income, and home value ranges. All of these can be even broken down by US States.

If there is something you would like to see on this site, please fill out a feedback form on the Contact page. We look forward to helping you find the best place for you to call home.

Check Out These Top Rated Cities

Chesapeake, VA

Living in Chesapeake offers residents a sparse suburban feel and most residents own their homes. In Chesapeake there are a lot of parks. Many families and young professionals live in Chesapeake. The public schools in Chesapeake are highly rated.

Colorado Springs, CO

There are many Veterans and Military that live in Colorado Springs. Living in Colorado Springs offers residents a dense suburban feel and most residents own their homes. In Colorado Springs there are a lot of coffee shops and parks.The public schools in Colorado Springs are above average.

Ft Wayne, IN

Fort Wayne is in Allen County and is one of the best places to live in Indiana. Living in Fort Wayne offers residents a dense suburban feel and most residents own their homes. In Fort Wayne there are a lot of coffee shops and parks. The public schools in Fort Wayne are above average.
