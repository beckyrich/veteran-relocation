About Us

This website was created during a weekend long Online Veteran Hackathon hosted by Fall In, 2-4 April 2021. "Fall In helps current and former US Military members build products and services that serve their own community through hackathons and events. Hackathons for veterans by veterans." The purpose of this project is to have one location for Military and Veterans to find places to live, to fit their unique needs.

George Clay served in the US Navy who....... George compiled the back end of this project and all of the databases.

Becky Richardson has been a Federal Contractor working for the US Army in the Soldier Recovery Unit at Ft Campbell. She has been offering Recreation Therapy to wounded, ill, and injured service members through Adaptive Reconditioning. Becky is a Front End Engineer specializing in JavaScript, Bootstrap, React, and React Native. She is learning the Back End through NodeJS, to become a Full Stack Engineer.
